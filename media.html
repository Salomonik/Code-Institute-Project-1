Media

Manga

"One Piece" manga, penned by Eiichiro Oda, seamlessly combines adventure, humor, and intricate storytelling as it chronicles the journey of Monkey D. Luffy and his diverse crew in search of the elusive treasure, One Piece, in a captivating world filled with mythical islands, powerful foes, and enduring camaraderie.

Games

The "One Piece" video games offer players the opportunity to immerse themselves in the vibrant world of Eiichiro Oda's creation, featuring engaging gameplay, dynamic battles, and the chance to relive key moments from the manga and anime. These games often showcase the Straw Hat Pirates' adventures, allowing fans to experience the thrill of exploring the Grand Line, battling iconic foes, and embracing the spirit of piracy in an interactive gaming experience.

Anime

The "One Piece" anime, adapted from Eiichiro Oda's manga, stands out as an enduring and beloved series known for its compelling narrative, diverse characters, and epic adventures. Spanning numerous episodes, the anime faithfully brings to life the journey of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew, exploring the vast and imaginative world of the Grand Line. With a perfect blend of action, humor, and heartfelt moments, the anime has garnered a global fanbase, captivating audiences with its rich storytelling and dynamic animation, making it a quintessential part of the anime landscape.

Netflix Adaptation

The live-action series is executive produced by series creator Eiichiro Oda (also known as Oda-Sensei), created in partnership with Shueisha, and produced by Tomorrow Studios and Netflix. Matt Owens and Steven Maeda are writers, executive producers, and showrunners. Marty Adelstein and Becky Clements also executive produce.
